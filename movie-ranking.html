The Best Movies According to Davina

My top 3 movies of all-time

Aladdin.2019

Aladdin is a visually stunning live-action remake of the original Aladdin. It blends adventure, romance and humor while celebrating cultural richness. It teaches us to be true to ourselves, that honesty and self-worth matter more than appearance and status, and that freedom is a gift worh fightingfor.

Girls Trip

This is a hilarious heartfelt comedy about four lifelong friends reconnecting during a wild trip to New Orleans. It teaches us the value of true friendship, being unapologetically yourself, and that healing often begins when surrounded by people who love and upflit you.

Harry Potter and The Prisoner of Azkaban

This Harry Potter is the darkest and most visually distinct of the early Harry Potter films. The story deepens emotional complexity, introducing rich characters, and explores time travelin a clever, satisfying way. It teaches us that things arent always what they seem, that understanding someone's past can change your entire perception of them, and that courage often means facing the truth-even when its painful.
